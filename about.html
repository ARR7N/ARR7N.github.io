About


Why Play Connect 4?

There's more to Connect 4 than merely matching discs. It's an entertaining way to develop critical thinking skills, a contest of cunning, and a dance of strategy and foresight. There's a spot for you here whether your goal is to learn the ins and outs of strategic play or just to have a fun match.


Features

  • Turn-Based Gameplay: Battle your friends by taking turns on the same device, making it the perfect game for casual entertainment.
  • Reset Button: Angry? No winner in sight? simply hit the reset button and start fresh.
  • User Interface: Our interface is as simple as you would like, simply enter your name and click a colour... TO BATTLE.
  • Scoreboard: Keep track of your hard battles with your friends with the scoreboard feature, casual or competitive it is always fun to rub it in their face.

Education Benefits

Connect 4 certainly is enternaining however it is a tool that can be used to develop critical thinking and improve problem solving skills. By learning to anticipate your opponents next move and planning steps ahead, this helps develop strategic thinking. This game is a great way to sharpen your mind, enhance concentraion and improve your patience.
