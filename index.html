How to Play

Gameplay

  • The objective is to get 4 of your own coloured discs in a row. Rows may be Diagonal, vertical, or horizontal.
  • Each player drops one of their discs into any one of the 7 columns in turn. The disc will go in the lowest point of the column.
  • Players must strategise blocking their opponents moves while working on creating their line of 4

How to Win

  • The game is won by the first player to succesfully connect four discs in a row.
